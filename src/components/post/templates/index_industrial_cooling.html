Standards & Thresholds

Temperature Uniformity (EN 12830 / HACCP)

TNU target (refrigeration) < 2°C
TNU target (freezing) < 3°C
Refrigeration setpoint 0 – 8°C (product dependent)
Freezing setpoint −30 – −18°C (product dependent)

Airflow Performance

Stagnation target < 15% (U < 0.10 m/s)
High-velocity limit < 5% (U > 2.0 m/s)
VUI target > 0.60
ADE target > 0.70

Air Changes per Hour — ACH

Refrigeration (0–8°C) 40–60 h⁻¹
Freezing (−30 to −18°C) 20–40 h⁻¹
Below target Insufficient air renewal
Above target Excess energy consumption

Energy Metrics

SEC — Refrigeration 3.0–5.0 kWh/m³/year (typical)
SEC — Freezing 8.0–15.0 kWh/m³/year (typical)
Q_infiltration Gosney-Olama analytical model
COP reference Refrig: 3.0–4.5  |  Freeze: 1.5–2.5
